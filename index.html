Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Order of 86? ▼

The Order of 86 is a collection of unique wizard dogs, each with their own magical abilities, personality, and backstory across six different mystical orders.

How many orders are there? ▼

There are six mystical orders: Flame (fire/forge), Radiant (light/healing), Deep (prophecy/truth), Wild (nature/growth), Arcane (knowledge/theory), and Heart (bonds/emotion).

What makes each wizard unique? ▼

Each wizard has unique traits including fur color, pattern, eyes, clothing, realm origin, magical order, and a detailed backstory with suggested name and lore.
